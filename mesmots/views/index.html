Documentation

Filters:

- Starts with: 

startswith("x")

- - - example: 

startswith("b§Z")

- Ends with: 

endswith("x")

- - - example: 

endswith("Et")

- Contains: 

contains("x")

- - - example: 

contains("Et")

Operators:

- And: 

&
Example: 
filter & filter

- Or: 

|
Example: 
filter | filter

- Xor: 

^
Example: 
filter ^ filter

Examples:

- Basic example: 

startswith("ba")

- With operators: 

(startswith("ba") & endswith("Et"))

- With operators: 

(startswith("ba") | startswith("b§Z"))

- With two operators: 

((startswith("ba") | startswith("b§Z")) & endswith("Et"))

startswith("x") endswith("x") contains("x")
